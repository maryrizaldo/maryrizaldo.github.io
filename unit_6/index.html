My name is Mary R. and I am a junior architect in New York City, but more importantly I am a curious citizen of the world with a desire to understand both the familiar and unfamiliar surrounding we live in. I want to share, with you all, what I have discovered from my travels here in - THRESHOLD.

What is a Threshold?

In architectural jargon, we often used the term threshold in a tedious, technical detail, almost seemingly boring even. But even something so technical, can make for a sensorial experience; the smallest of details can make the difference for experiencing your space and surrounding. Essentially, a threshold can be defined in a more general way as a point of entry or beginning.
