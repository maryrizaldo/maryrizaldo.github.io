Who am I?

My name is Mary Rizaldo and I am a junior architect in New York City, but more importantly I am a curious citizen of the world with a desire to understand both familiar and unfamiliar surrounding we live in. I want to share with you all what I have learned from my travels here in - THRESHOLD.

What is Threshold?

In architectural jargon, we often used the term threshold in a technical detail in a seemingly almost banal way. But, even the smallest of details can make the difference for the experiencing your space and surrounding. But threshold can also be defined in a more generally as a point of entry or beginning. Here, we will define threshold as a point of entry or beginning.
